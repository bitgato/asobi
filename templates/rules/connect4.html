Rules

Players Two

Description

Players take turns in dropping stones on a grid subject to gravity. The first player to get four stones in a row wins.

The game is usually played on a 7 x 6 grid. However, other board sizes are avaliable, including 5 x 4, 6 x 5, 8 x 7, 9 x 7, 10 x 7 and 8 x 8.

Gameplay

The game creator has red stones and the opponent has yellow stones.

Both players take turns dropping stones into columns of their choice by clicking the Drop button on the top of the column. A stone cannot be dropped into a column which has been filled to the top.

The game continues until one of the players has connected four of his stones in a row (vertical, horizontal, or diagonal).
